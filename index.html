Before you come to collect your jacket
Please follow these three short rules.
1
It is mandatory to bring your student ID card. It is also strongly recommended that you know your order number to make pickup much faster.
2
If you cannot come yourself, send someone on your behalf with your order number or student ID. Items may not be held otherwise.
3
Come only at your assigned period time to avoid crowding and to let everyone collect their jackets comfortably. If you arrive before or after your period, you will not be able to collect your items. After receiving your jacket, please move to the college yard so the pickup area stays clear.
