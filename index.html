Before you come to collect your jacket
Please follow these three short rules.
1
Bring your student ID card if your college requires it, and make sure you know your order number. Having the order number ready makes pickup much faster.
2
If you cannot come yourself, send someone on your behalf with your order number or student ID. Items may not be held otherwise.
3
Come only at your assigned period time. If you arrive before or after your period, you will not be able to collect your items.
